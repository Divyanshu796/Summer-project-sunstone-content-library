About Us

Our Story

Welcome to KnowleJ, your haven for book lovers! We're a team of passionate readers and learners dedicated to providing a vast collection of books for our community. Our mission is to foster a love for reading and make knowledge accessible to everyone.

Our journey began in 2023 with a small collection of books and a big dream. Over the years, we've grown to become a thriving book library, thanks to the contributions of our members, volunteers, and staff. Today, we're proud to offer a vast collection of titles, regular events, and a welcoming space for readers of all ages.

Our Vision

KnowleJ was founded by Harsh Vardhan, a book lover and educator who envisioned a space where people could come together to share their passion for reading. With a humble beginning in 2024, our library has grown to become a beloved institution in the community, thanks to the support of our patrons and the dedication of our team.
